Privacy Policy
-----------------------------

By linking your Patreon account on "xblazegmd.vercel.app/patreon/link", your Patreon access token and Geometry Dash Account ID both get stored in a database, so the supporter badge can be shown

If you don't like this, you can always unlink your account by going to the settings of the "Xblaze's Geode API" mod and unlinking your Patreon account there
